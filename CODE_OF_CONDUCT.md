# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse,
inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being kind, patient, and respectful.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.
- Showing empathy towards other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, and personal or political
  attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualised language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the project maintainer by opening a confidential issue or via the
contact channels listed on the repository. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the
privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

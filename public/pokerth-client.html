PokerTH Web Client
– Le jeu de Poker Texas Holdem par excellence
– Partie solo avec possibilité de jouer jusqu'à 9 adversaires contrôlés par l'ordinateur
– Parties sur Internet et sur serveur privé
– Mode entraînement hors ligne contre les bots

Privacy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Who runs this

The PokerTH Web Client is a free and open-source hobby project, maintained by an individual, Arnaud Obscur ("narmod"), in collaboration with the PokerTH project. It is not a company and has no commercial purpose: no ads, no sale of data, no advertising profiling, no third-party trackers.

What stays in your browser

Your settings (theme, language, table styles, sound options...) are stored locally in your browser and never leave your device. You can clear them at any time from your browser settings.

What the server sees

  • Anonymous visit counters: a random identifier stored in your browser is used to count distinct devices and visits. These statistics contain no name and no IP address and are deleted after about 13 months.
  • Technical connection data: like any web server, this one receives the connection data (including your IP address) needed to deliver and protect the service. It is used for nothing else.
  • Game data: if you play on a ranked server, the nickname you choose and your game results are recorded to display the leaderboard.

Third-party services

When you connect to pokerth.net (or any other game server), you are using a service operated independently of this web client, with its own rules. This page only covers the web client and the server that hosts it.

Chat translation: if you tap the translate button (🌐) on a chat message, the text of that message is sent to Google's translation service (Google Translate) so it can be translated into your language. Nothing is sent unless you tap the button, and the feature can be turned off in Advanced options.

Free software - AGPL-3.0

The entire client is free software, released under the GNU AGPL v3 license. You can read the source code, verify what it does, host your own copy, and modify it:

PokerTH itself is released under the GNU GPL license.

Your rights and contact
